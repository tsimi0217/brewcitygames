Biography

I have been playing video games since I was 3. The first gaming system I started playing on was an Atari. The first game I remember playing was a bird game, linking logs together to create ladders. Growing up, we had an Apple II GS, and I would play Oregon Trail. My favorite game was Mario Brothers for Nintendo. This was the first ever Mario Brothers. I really enjoyed playing that game. Jak and Daxter is another game I really enjoyed. Now, I really enjoy playing Texas Hold 'Em and The Room. I really wish there were more games similar to The Room. I really would like games that are mind challenging and thought provoking.
